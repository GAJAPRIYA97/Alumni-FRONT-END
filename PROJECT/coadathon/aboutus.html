Our Story

QuickServe was founded in 2022 by a team of construction industry veterans who recognized a fundamental problem: connecting skilled workers with the right jobs was inefficient, time-consuming, and often based on word-of-mouth rather than verified skills and experience.

After witnessing skilled workers struggle to find consistent work and homeowners facing challenges in finding reliable professionals, our founders set out to create a platform that would streamline this process while prioritizing quality, trust, and fair opportunities.

Starting with just a handful of workers and customers in one city, QuickServe has grown to serve multiple regions, connecting thousands of skilled professionals with customers who value their expertise. Our verification system ensures that customers can trust the workers they hire, while our commitment to fair opportunities means that workers of all backgrounds can find meaningful employment.

QuickServe continues to evolve,driven by our own mission to transform the construction andcicil work industry through technology and a communiuty centered-approach.
